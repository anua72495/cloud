ABOUT THE COMPANY

GS Construction has crossed over three decades and emerged as successful, reputed and trusted Mechanical and Piping Contractor in India, unfailingly providing quality services in Thermal Power Generation, Hydrocarbon /Oil & Gas and Petrochemical Sectors. The company was started by Shri Gopal Singh as Proprietor firm in 1976 and was eventually converted to Private Limited in 2010. M/s GS Construction now seeks to expand their business profile to implement Projects on EPC mode in Thermal Power Generation, Hydrocarbon /Oil & Gas and Petrochemical and Infrastructure sector. GS Construction is supported by experienced team of professionals with good managerial skills, adequate technical manpower, machineries, finance to execute the project well in time maintaining best of quality and professionalism. Our Major Activities Pipelines in carbon/stainless/alloy steel, Rotary and stationary equipments installation, Fire Fighting, Fire Alarm and Foam System, Structural Steel work for bunkers/towers, Heat tracing with Hot & Cold Insulation, Sandblasting, Painting, Process & Utility Systems installation, Horizontal and Vertical Storage Tanks in Carbon Steel and Stainless Steel.

RESOURCES

GS Construction understands that a robust company is not a collection of 'human resources', it is a community of engaged human beings. Our people, wherever located, are our true strength. We consider them to be one of our key assets and owe our success to their dedication, expertise, and enthusiasm. Their caliber is the fuel for our growth. And, as we stand poised to scale greater heights, we look forward to having an even stronger and mutually rewarding relationship with them. At all our projects, team spirit among workers has contributed to precise co-ordination, motivation and enthusiasm. The dynamic synergy of our resource has been the primary catalyst in the steady growth of GS Construction.

We believe that no business succeeds without innovative ideas and motivated employees. GS construction believes that the best way to remain ahead means respecting and applying the ideas, values and thoughts from a group of employees and customers

We are dedicated to the development of the expertise and know-how of our employees. Our personnel policies are aimed towards recruiting talented employees, and encouraging the development of their skills.
